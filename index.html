Places I've visited around the world

Paris, France

Visited the Eiffel Tower and enjoyed croissants at a local cafe. The architecture and ambiance were breathtaking.

Explored ancient temples and strolled through beautiful cherry blossom-lined streets. A perfect mix of tradition and nature.

New York City, USA

Walked through Times Square, enjoyed a Broadway show, and had the best pizza ever. The city that never sleeps!
